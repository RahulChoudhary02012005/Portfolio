3D Modeling

Description:
On second day We learnt how to convert 2d designs in 3d designs using extrude tool and change the appearence of the objects.
Extrude tool in Fusion 360 to convert 2D designs in 3D models.
Apearance tool in Fusion 360 is used to give different colors, textures, and designs to the models.
We also learnt about fillet tool to use it to smoothen the sharpen corners and edges of 3D objects.

We turned all are 3d projects in orthographic projection.

Steps to Show Orthographic Projection of 3D Models over the Drawing Sheets

1.) Choose the 3d model of which we have to make the Orthographic projection
2.) Go to Design option and select "From Design" option in drawing option.
3.) Then place the objects over the sheet as per requirement and select the view which you want to Draw over the sheet.
4.) To give the dimensions to the drawing go to the auto dimension option and select the way you want the dimensions to be viewed

Servo Motor

A servo motor is a type of rotary actuator that allows precise control of angular position, velocity, and acceleration. It consists of a motor coupled with a feedback mechanism that senses the position of the motor shaft.
